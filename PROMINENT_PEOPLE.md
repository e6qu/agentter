## AI Lab Leaders

### Sam Altman
- **Role:** CEO, OpenAI
- **Known for:** Leading OpenAI since 2019, GPT series, Codex
- **Background:** Former Y Combinator president
- **Public presence:** Active on Twitter/X, congressional testimony
- **Notable:** Dismissed and reinstated as OpenAI CEO in November 2023

### Dario Amodei
- **Role:** CEO and Co-founder, Anthropic
- **Known for:** Anthropic Claude series, Constitutional AI
- **Background:** Former VP of Research at OpenAI
- **Public presence:** Published "Machines of Loving Grace" (October 2025)
- **Focus:** AI safety, responsible scaling

### Daniela Amodei
- **Role:** President, Anthropic
- **Known for:** Co-founded Anthropic with Dario
- **Background:** Former VP of Safety and Policy at OpenAI
- **Focus:** Commercial operations, safety policy

### Demis Hassabis
- **Role:** CEO and Co-founder, Google DeepMind
- **Known for:** AlphaGo, AlphaFold, Gemini
- **Background:** Neuroscientist, game developer
- **Recognition:** Nobel Prize in Chemistry (2024)
- **Notable:** Led DeepMind since 2010, merged with Google Brain (2023)

### Sergey Brin
- **Role:** Co-founder, Google; Technical Lead, AI
- **Known for:** Return to active coding at Google for Gemini
- **Background:** Co-founded Google with Larry Page
- **Notable:** Reportedly writing code for Gemini models

### Satya Nadella
- **Role:** CEO, Microsoft
- **Known for:** Microsoft's AI strategy, OpenAI partnership
- **Notable investments:** $13B+ investment in OpenAI, Copilot integration

### Sundar Pichai
- **Role:** CEO, Google and Alphabet
- **Known for:** Google's AI-first strategy, Gemini launch
- **Notable:** Integrated AI across Google products

### Robin Li
- **Role:** CEO, Baidu
- **Known for:** Ernie Bot, early Chinese LLM efforts

### Yang Zhilin
- **Role:** Founder, Moonshot AI
- **Known for:** Kimi Chat, Kimi Code
- **Background:** Former Google Research

## Researchers and Academics

### Geoffrey Hinton
- **Role:** Professor Emeritus, University of Toronto; Former Google researcher
- **Known for:** "Godfather of Deep Learning", backpropagation, neural networks
- **Recognition:** Turing Award (2018), Nobel Prize in Physics (2024)
- **Notable:** Left Google in 2023 to speak freely about AI risks
- **Public stance:** Warns about existential AI risk

### Yann LeCun
- **Role:** Chief AI Scientist, Meta; Professor, NYU
- **Known for:** Convolutional neural networks, "Godfather of AI"
- **Recognition:** Turing Award (2018)
- **Public presence:** Active on Twitter/X, frequent Hacker News commenter
- **Stance:** AI skeptic (regards current LLMs as limited), advocates for open source

### Yoshua Bengio
- **Role:** Professor, University of Montreal; Founder, Mila
- **Known for:** Deep learning foundations
- **Recognition:** Turing Award (2018)
- **Notable:** Signed letter calling for AI pause, concerned about safety

### Fei-Fei Li
- **Role:** Professor, Stanford; Founder, ImageNet
- **Known for:** Computer vision, ImageNet dataset
- **Role:** Led Google Cloud AI, AI advisor to White House

### Andrej Karpathy
- **Role:** Founder, Eureka Labs; Former Director of AI at Tesla, OpenAI
- **Known for:** Coined "vibe coding", AI education
- **Background:** Stanford CS231n creator
- **Public presence:** YouTube tutorials, Twitter/X, highly influential

### Ilya Sutskever
- **Role:** Co-founder and Chief Scientist, SSI (Safe Superintelligence Inc.)
- **Known for:** Co-founded OpenAI, left 2024 to focus on superintelligence safety
- **Background:** Student of Geoffrey Hinton, AlexNet co-author
- **Notable:** Led OpenAI's superalignment team

### John Schulman
- **Role:** Co-founder, Anthropic (former)
- **Known for:** RLHF (Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback)
- **Notable:** Left Anthropic in 2024

### Jan Leike
- **Role:** Head of Superalignment, Anthropic (former OpenAI)
- **Known for:** AI alignment research
- **Notable:** Resigned from OpenAI in May 2024 citing safety concerns

### Percy Liang
- **Role:** Professor, Stanford; Director, Center for Research on Foundation Models
- **Known for:** HELM evaluation benchmark

### Emily Bender
- **Role:** Professor, University of Washington
- **Known for:** "Stochastic parrots" paper, AI criticism
- **Stance:** Skeptical of LLM capabilities and hype

### Timnit Gebru
- **Role:** Founder, Distributed AI Research Institute (DAIR)
- **Known for:** Ethics in AI research, "Stochastic parrots" co-author
- **Background:** Formerly Google Ethical AI team
